What we are about

Established in 2006, Meskay & Femtee Trading Company (Pvt) Ltd (MFT) is a Grain trading, milling and processing company specializing in Rice and Wheat among other grains. Since inception MFTC has been delivering unparalleled quality while upholding the highest standards of business ethics.

History

The company draws its heritage from one of the pioneer business families of Pakistan and were the first flour millers in Pakistan under the auspices of India Flour Mills (1936) (Pvt) Ltd. They have owned and operated various businesses that include: India Flour Mills (1936) (Pvt) Ltd., Pan Islamic Steamship Company Ltd., Digri Cotton Ginning & Pressing Factory (Pvt) Ltd., Mardi Metal Foundries and Mercantile Fire & General Insurance Company Ltd. Through the leadership of the CEO, MFTC has successfully been propelled to the great heights of success, securing various awards and recognitions since inception.
