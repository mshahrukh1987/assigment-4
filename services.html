Services - From Farm To Plate

MFTC covers the entire supply chain of rice from cultivation to procurement (Punjab & Sindh), milling, grading, packing and shipment processes. We maintain a complete database of where our goods are purchased and have mapped out our supply locations and stations. Bag-mark development and preparations of various types of packing material for clients.

Procurement

Our procurement offices in the Sindh and Punjab provinces provide finance to farmers of rice paddy including supply of fertilizers, pesticides and other inputs.

We also extend expert advice on modern and efficient farming techniques, so as to ensure higher yields and profitability for our suppliers.

Milling & Processing

Paddy procured is milled at our selected mills in the two provinces. The mills undergo a stringent in-house audit before being empaneled by us. Our qualified inspectors superintend the milling processes at the mills, so as to ensure quality production, minimal wastages and losses as well as product and by product accounting.

Products requiring reprocessing according to buyers’ requirements are either mill processed in our Karachi mill or manually sieved in the case of the medium and lower qualities. Mill processed goods are cleaned and any remaining paddy is removed; even the smallest foreign matter is removed from the goods. Then these goods are polished as per the buyer's requirement, graded by length, width and color and packed in bags as per buyer's requirements.

Packaging & Shipment

Packing for export is then conducted at which point our export operation team handles transportation to the port and shipment into containers or on board the vessel as the case may be. Through our associate companies in East Africa and the Middle East, we also stock and sell high quality and branded rice for our customers in these markets.

Farming

At MFTC our aim is to build a mutually beneficial relationship with the farmer; the main source of our products. With a dedicated unit that rents out farm machinery to farmers mainly harvesters, laser levelers and tractors. Along with introducing transplanting and automated spraying options.

With over 8200 Acres of land on lease for farming and 75 acres for seed production we aspire to become the partner for our farmers. Partners that help them grow and prosper in turn helping us produce the finest quality products for our local and international customers.

We pride ourselves in being present at all stages of rice from farming to the time it reaches your plate and that is the guarantee to Meskay & Femtee’s Seal of Quality.
